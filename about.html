Cinematographer, Editor

Suraj Bista

Suraj Bista is a cinematographer, independent filmmaker and editor. Suraj grew up in the valley of Kathmandu, Nepal with the backdrop of stunning Himalayas. At the age of 15, he moved to Boulder, Colorado. In high school, he was part of the team responsible for creating weekly announcements as well as creative videos. That's when he fell in love with filmmaking.

He is currently based and working out of Denver, Colorado. He got his BFA in Film & Television at the University of Colorado Denver. With a diverse set of experience, he works to help bring director's visions into life. He has worked as a cinematographer in award winning films like Seventh Wolf, Talion and Koselig.
